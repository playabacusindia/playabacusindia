ABOUT US?

Ideal Play Abacus India Private Limited

(IPA) was founded in 2003 by women entrepreneurs to develop the mathematical and brain skills of young children using the magic toolabacus. Over the past 18 years, IPA has expanded across several states in India and abroad, offering abacus maths and abacus mathematics programs. With over 1500++ centers across India and more than 4,00,000+ students, IPA is a trusted name in abacus education. We also offer programs in countries like the USA, UAE, Australia, South Africa, and more, making abacus learning accessible worldwide. Explore the power of abacus mastermind, abacus Global, and childs abacus training with IPA.

ISO 9001: 2015 Certified.

  • One of the primary institutions founded by an all-women entrepreneur team in 2003, Ideal Play Abacus India Private Limited (IPA) has grown into a leading provider of abacus education.
  • With over 1500++ centers in India, 2000+ trained teachers, and more than 4,00,000 enthusiastic students, IPA is at the forefront of abacus maths and abacus mastermind training.
  • IPA is proud to be the first Indian company to receive the Premier Achievement Training certification from the Chinese Abacus Association. We are also the only Indian company to offer advanced abacus mathematics courses, including Square Root and Decimal Multiplication.
  • We host certified national-level competitions annually and conduct international grading exams through Guang Xi Abacus Association Malaysia.

Mrs. Shaarada K.Sriram

MANAGING DIRECTOR

A qualified Chartered Accountant and Cost Accountant, she is the Managing Director of Ideal Play Abacus India Pvt Ltd. Academically, she ranked All India 10th in Chartered Accountancy, was a gold medalist in ICWA (Institute of Cost and Works Accountants of India), and earned a gold medal in her Bachelors degree (B.Com). She also secured State 2nd position in the 12th Standard School Leaving Exam. Additionally, she was named the Best Outgoing Student of Stella Maris College and represented India at the Commonwealth Games Closing Ceremony in New Zealand.

She began her career as a Funds Manager at Unit Trust of India and ventured into the abacus business 16 years ago. With her expertise in business affairs and deep knowledge of the abacus education sector, she successfully leads the company, providing quality abacus maths and abacus mastermind programs across India and beyond.
