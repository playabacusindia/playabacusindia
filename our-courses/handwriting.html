Handwriting

HANDWRITING PRACTICE

IPAs handwriting program focuses on essential skills for cursive writing through basic exercises like figure-ground discrimination and sequencing for consistent penmanship.

Advantages of IPA Handwriting Practice Program

The IPA handwriting practice program helps improve legibility, avoid mistakes, and ensures neat presentation, making it ideal for both right- and left-handed children.

COURSE DETAILS

  • The programme is for the age group of 5 years and above.

Level Details:

  • Sub Junior: 5 years to 6 years.
  • Junior: 7 years to 9 years.
  • Senior: 9 years and Above.
  • Calligraphy: 9 years and Above.

Session details:

  • Duration for 4 levels - 3 Months each level.
  • Weekly one class of 2 hours each.
  • International Certificate are awarded on the successful completion of each level.

  • BAG
  • T-SHIRT
  • BOOKS
