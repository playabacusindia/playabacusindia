Math Adventures at Brain Tree

Brain Tree Math Fun

At Brain Tree, kids enjoy learning math through activities like addition, subtraction, and fractions, making math easy and exciting.

The objectives of using the tool are as follows:

  • To learn and develop a strong number sense in basic math.
  • To understand properties of addition and subtraction.
  • To recognize patterns and relationships among numbers and fractions.
  • To enhance memory and mental skills through building concepts in algebraic expressions and Vedic maths.
  • To develop algebraic thinking and problem-solving abilities with math puzzles and 2D and 3D shapes.

    • The colourful and student-friendly device presents the number sequence on slideable and rotatable blocks that serve as the reckoning slide on the top row, and provides a set of movable blocks on the bottom slide that are aligned neatly with the blocks on the top row in matching colors to help with counting and solving math problems. This design helps build a maths foundation in children and improve classroom performance by:

    Combining all modes of learning kinesthetic, tactile, and visual this tool is designed to enhance maths learning. It minimizes errors associated with loose pieces and the use of fingers, helping children visualize patterns and relationships with ease. Children can explore one or more numbers on a daily basis, mastering addition facts of each number and learning various combinations of arriving at the number in a systematic way.

    Once the children are taught the use of the tool for the first few numbers, they can use it for learning other numbers and develop a strong number sense. The repeated use of the tool will give them a clear understanding of the numbers, thereby enabling them to memorize addition and subtraction facts with ease, encouraging them to enjoy math. Additionally, the use of the tool also helps in laying the foundation of mental arithmetic.
