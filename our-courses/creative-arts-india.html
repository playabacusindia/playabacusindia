Some Fascinating Facts on Arts:

  • Students who study art are 4 times more likely to be recognised for academic achievement.
  • New research shows that art promotes creativity, self-worth, and social development.
  • Art programmes are mandatory in countries that rank the highest for math and science.
  • Arts teach children that problems can have many solutions, fostering critical thinking.
  • Drawing develops hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness abilities that engineers and architects use.
  • Art develops kids' imagination and critical thinking skills.
  • Teaching students to be creative is a deliberate process, much like teaching reading or math.
  • Learning to draw and think creatively helps prepare students for the future, especially in art and craft.
  • Art teaches kids to imagine more possibilities, enriching their problem-solving abilities.

Development Benefits of Arts:

  • Motor skills: Arts help develop fine motor skills through activities like drawing and painting, improving hand-eye coordination.
  • Language development: Engaging in art encourages children to express themselves, expanding vocabulary and communication skills.
  • Decision making: Art promotes problem-solving, helping children make decisions as they work through creative processes.
  • Visual learning: Children develop visual learning skills, enhancing their ability to interpret and understand visual information.
  • Inventiveness: Art stimulates creativity and encourages children to think outside the box, developing their inventiveness.
  • Cultural awareness: Through art, children learn about different cultures, fostering appreciation for diversity and global perspectives.
  • Improved academic performance: Research shows that children involved in art perform better academically, enhancing skills in math, science, and literacy.
