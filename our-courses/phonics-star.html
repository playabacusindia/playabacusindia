Phonics Star - U.K. ENGLISH

Our U.K. English programme is a research-based, comprehensive course that teaches students effective use of English, focusing on phonics, fluency, and Received Pronunciation (the Queens English). Designed for young learners, this course ensures they acquire the necessary skills to speak, read, and write confidently.

What does phonics mean?

Phonics is a method of teaching reading that emphasizes the relationship between letters and sounds, essentially helping children learn how to decode words by connecting letters to sounds.

  • There are 26 letters
  • There are 44 sounds

We blend sounds together to help us read (build words up).

We segment words into sounds to help us spell (break words down).

Multi-sensory teaching

Each sound is taught in a multisensory (story, song, and action) way to enable children to recognize:

  • Sound
  • Letter name
  • Shape
  • Formation
